Key features

The story

  • You awaken in a cursed fortress haunted by a horse-head demon.
  • Navigate crypts, halls, and towers while avoiding supernatural horrors.
  • Collect keys through clues, visions, and ghostly encounters.
  • Your choices determine whether you break the curse or become another lost soul.

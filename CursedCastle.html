Cursed Castle

Horror Adventure | Puzzle Design | Unity 3D

A dark fantasy horror adventure game set in a haunted medieval castle. Explore treacherous corridors, solve ancient puzzles, and face supernatural entities as you uncover the castle's cursed secrets and attempt to break the dark spell that binds lost souls.

The Story

You awaken in the depths of Unknown Castle, a fortress cursed centuries ago by a HorseHead Demon
Navigate through crypts, great halls, and forbidden towers while avoiding supernatural horrors
Piece together the Keys through Clues, visions, and ghostly encounters
Your choices determine whether you break the curse or become another lost soul
